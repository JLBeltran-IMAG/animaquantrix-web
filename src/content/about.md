<!-- lang:en -->
Animaquantrix is currently developed as an initiative whose earliest visible form emerged in 2017 under the name Spin Charge Group Meeting (SCGM), founded and led by Professor Dr.C. Leo Diago Cisneros at the Department of Physics and Mathematics of Universidad Iberoamericana, Santa Fe campus, Mexico City.

Its foundations integrate the work carried out by Dr.C. Diago before 2017 at the Faculty of Physics of the University of Havana and in other international institutions. From the SCGM, and from the initiative in its current stage, have emerged diploma works, degree projects, scientific articles, book chapters, and conference reports.

In its present version, the composition is more heterogeneous and the topics are broader, with emphasis on research lines of stronger scientific and technological impact.

Diago en la Facultad de Fisica de la Universidad de La Habana, y en otras instituciones extranjeras.

Del SCGM y del actual NDGM, han salido trabajos de diploma, proyectos de titulacion, articulos cientificos, capitulos de libros y reportes en congresos.

En su actual version, o sea NDGM, la composicion es mas heterogenea, los temas mas amplios, con enfasis en topicos de mayor impacto cientifico-tecnologico.
